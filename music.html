About Me

Born and raised in Boston, USA, I started learning violin from Vid. Smt. Tara Anand at age 8. After relocating to Bangalore in 2009, I trained under Vid. Smt. Lalgudi Rajalakshmi and Vid. Sri Yashasvi, and am currently under the tutelage of Legendary Padmasri A. Kanyakumari.

Together with my brother Tejas Manjunath — known as the Boston Brothers — we have performed over 700 violin concerts across India, USA, and Singapore, sharing the stage with our teacher Vid. A. Kanyakumari. We have won accolades including 1st place Violin Duet at Sangeeth Samraat (Sankara TV, Chennai), Pratibhakankshi award, and Nada Chintamani. Notable venues include Rama Seva Mandali, Brahma Gana Sabha, Chowdiah Memorial Hall, Gayana Samaja, Shanmukhananda Hall (Bombay), Puttaparthi–Prashanti Nilayam, and the Chennai December Music Season.

Featured in The Hindu, Indian Express, Deccan Herald, DD Chandana, Zee Kannada, and Colors Kannada. In 2018 I obtained First Rank in the Vidwath Music Examination (Karnataka State Music Board).
